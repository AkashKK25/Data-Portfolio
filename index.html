Publications

Utilizing High-Resolution Imaging and Artificial Intelligence for Accurate Leaf Wetness Detection for the Strawberry Advisory System (SAS)

Authors: Kondaparthi, A.K.; Lee, W.S.; Peres, N.A.
Journal: Sensors 2024, 24, 4836
Published: June 2024

This research introduces a novel approach combining high-resolution imaging techniques and artificial intelligence algorithms to accurately detect leaf wetness, a critical parameter in the Strawberry Advisory System (SAS). The system achieves 94.7% accuracy in real-world conditions, significantly improving disease prediction and management strategies for strawberry cultivation.
